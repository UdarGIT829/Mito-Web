How comparison options work

Out of all mutations across all samples in the study, this tool allows you to limit the scope to seeing common and unique mutations among samples.

Present

The mutation must occur in this sample. With multiple Present samples, it must occur in all of them.

Unique

Include mutations unique to this sample. Unique choices are added to the results alongside the Present intersection.

Not in

Exclude any mutation found in this sample. Not in is exclusive for that sample.

Common combinations

  • Present A + Present B: mutations shared by both.
  • Present A + Not in B: mutations in A but absent from B.
  • Unique A + Unique B: mutations unique to either sample.
  • Present + Unique on A: include A in the shared set and also include mutations unique to A.
